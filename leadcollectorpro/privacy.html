Privacy Policy for Lead Collector Pro

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Lead Collector Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Business contact information (names, email addresses, phone numbers) from web pages you visit
  • Website content necessary for contact information extraction

All data is stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Extract and organize business contacts
  • Store leads in your local database
  • Enable CSV export functionality
  • Provide basic lead management features

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • You maintain full control over your data
  • Data can be exported or deleted at any time

5. Third-Party Access

  • We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with external services
  • No analytics or tracking services are implemented

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Export your data
  • Delete your data
  • Control when the extension collects data

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of any material changes.

9. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, contact: support@webreachconnect.com

10. Compliance

We comply with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • GDPR requirements
  • Data protection regulations
